My Letters For You
Happy 6th Monthsary

"Hello, my love."

I am here for the second time, showing you what I know about being in love with you. I may not be a perfect person but you know that I always do my best. Being in love with you is the best feeling I could ever feel. I hope I spend all my tomorrows with you. Thank you for all the days that you made me feel loved and appreciated. You are my motivation. The reason and my everything. the on person I would chose at any cost. Every time I see your face and hear you voice, I can't help but to fall in love more. You are more meaningful than any other arts and songs. Simply a perfection that has ever existed. I am out of the words to tell how grateful I am to have you.

I have vouyage on all the sea and will travel around the galaxy. But no one else is more beautiful than you. You have brought so much light on my days than the moon and the sun up above us. I don't know what to do without you. You are beyond any measure. You have accepted every inch of me and thank you for that. I will love you until the stars no longer shines. 'Till dusk and dawn, I will be by your side. I am out of the words to tell how grateful I am to have you.

I am sorry if this is not that lengthy. It's just for now. You'll have another tomorrow. But I am placing all my love through this. Would you accept it? I hope you will.

I love you. Mahal kita
